EPISODE 1

Birmingham , 1919 Thomas Shelby controls the Peaky Blinders .one of the most feared criminal organisation or (gang) in England.When a crate of heavy guns went missing ,Thomas saw an oppurtinity to move up in the world At a church, Polly Gray pressures Thomas until he finally tells her about how his men accidentally acquired the wrong crate during a routine pick-up, instead taking a crate that contained Lewis machine guns, ammunition, semi-automatic rifles, and pistols with shells. Polly tells Thomas to throw away the machine guns before everyone else will find out that he is in possesion of them. Grace Burgess enters The Garrison Pub and talks to Harry Fenton, the landlord, about a job posting. At first Harry turns her down, but Grace sings part of a song (the Irish ballad “I wish I was in Carrickfergus”) that played him and accepted her offer . Arthur Shelby goes to the cinema with two women, but gets captured by policemen and taken to Chief Inspector Campbell. Campbell asks Arthur what he knows about the robbery, and beats him when Arthur does not give him the answers he wants,because Tommy didint even told Arthur about the guns yet . Arthur Shelby and Chief Inspector Campbell Grace and Harry help serve a full house of men on their way to a football game. Grace meets Thomas for the first time, and brings him a bottle of rum on the house. Harry warns her to stay away from him. The Shelby family helps patch Arthur up. Arthur tells Thomas that Campbell said Winston Churchill sent him to Birmingham on a case of national interest related to a robbery. Campbell told Arthur that he wants the Peaky Blinders to help him. Thomas says nothing, which irritates Arthur. In his bedroom, Thomas pours brown opium into his pipe and lights a match. He dreams an old memory of himself, Freddie and Danny in the tunnels during the War, trying to listen to the voices, before they get attacked. He wakes, looks out the window and sees two policemen walking down the street.
